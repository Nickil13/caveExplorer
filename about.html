About

I have been interested in caves for a long time. I find cave structures fascinating, from stalactites and stalagmites to soda straws. The various geological features and ecosystems inspire me and entice me to explore them.

The first cave I ever explored was the Rat's Nest Cave in Canmore, Alberta. I've also been to the Doolin Cave in County Clare, Ireland.
